EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT ("Agreement") is made effective as of 15th June 2025 between "TechNova Solutions Pvt. Ltd."", having its registered office at 302, Skyline Tower, Mumbai, India (the "Company"), and "Ankit Verma", residing at 14-A Orchid Enclave, Pune, Maharashtra, India (the "Employee").

BACKGROUND

The Company wishes to hire the Employee for his experience and technical expertise. The Employee is willing to accept such employment under the terms outlined in this Agreement.

1. COMMENCEMENT DATE AND TERM

The employment shall commence on 15th June 2025 for a fixed period of 18 months ending 15th December 2026. An initial probationary period of 3 months shall apply. Either party may terminate this Agreement in accordance with the terms stated herein or as provided by applicable law.

2. JOB TITLE AND DESCRIPTION

Job Title: Software Engineer – Web Applications

The Employee shall be responsible for full-stack web development, maintaining internal tools, writing clean code, participating in code reviews, and collaborating with cross-functional teams on various client projects.

3. COMPENSATION & BENEFITS

Monthly Salary: ₹45,000

Subject to annual performance reviews, the Employee shall be eligible for an increment ranging between 8% and 12% after the first 12 months of employment.

No paid leave shall be available during the probation period. Statutory deductions such as PF, TDS, and professional tax shall apply as per law.

4. WORK HOURS AND LOCATION

Location: Hybrid (3 days remote, 2 days in-office at Mumbai HQ)

Standard Working Hours: 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM IST. Extended hours may be required occasionally to meet project deadlines or support client deliverables.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY

The Employee agrees not to disclose any confidential or proprietary information, trade secrets, or business practices acquired during the course of employment, both during and after termination.

6. NON-COMPETITION

For a period of 12 months following the end of this Agreement, the Employee shall not work with or consult for any competitor or directly engage in similar business activities that may harm the interests of the Company.

7. TERMINATION

This Agreement may be terminated by either party with 30 days’ prior written notice or payment in lieu thereof. In the event of resignation during the first 6 months post-probation, the Employee shall forfeit one month's salary as penalty.

8. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed, interpreted, and enforced in accordance with the laws of the Republic of India. Disputes, if any, shall be subject to the jurisdiction of Mumbai courts.
