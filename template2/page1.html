InnovateHub Technologies Pvt. Ltd.
MANAGEMENT AGENCY

EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT ("Agreement") is made effective as of 1st July 2025 between "InnovateHub Technologies Pvt. Ltd.", having its registered office at 15th Floor, Tech Plaza, Sector 44, Gurgaon, Haryana, India (the "Company"), and "Priya Sharma", residing at B-204 Green Valley Apartments, Noida, Uttar Pradesh, India (the "Employee").

BACKGROUND

The Company is an innovative technology startup focused on AI-driven solutions and wishes to hire the Employee for their exceptional technical skills and product development expertise. The Employee is willing to accept such employment and contribute to the Company's mission of revolutionizing digital experiences.

1. COMMENCEMENT DATE AND TERM

The employment shall commence on 1st July 2025 for a fixed period of 24 months ending 30th June 2027. An initial probationary period of 6 months shall apply to assess mutual compatibility and performance standards. Either party may terminate this Agreement in accordance with the terms stated herein or as provided by applicable Indian labor laws.

2. JOB TITLE AND DESCRIPTION

Job Title: Senior Full Stack Developer

The Employee shall be responsible for developing and maintaining web applications using modern JavaScript frameworks (React, Node.js), designing scalable backend architectures and RESTful APIs, collaborating with cross-functional teams including designers, product managers, and DevOps, implementing automated testing strategies and CI/CD pipelines, mentoring junior developers and contributing to technical documentation, and participating in code reviews while maintaining high coding standards.

3. COMPENSATION & BENEFITS

Monthly Salary: ₹85,000

Performance Incentives: Subject to annual performance reviews, the Employee shall be eligible for salary increments ranging between 15% and 25% after the first 12 months, based on individual performance and company growth metrics.

Equity Participation: Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP) of 0.2% equity vesting over 4 years with a 1-year cliff period.

Benefits Package includes health insurance coverage for employee and family, annual learning & development budget of ₹25,000, flexible work arrangements and wellness programs, performance bonuses and team outing allowances.

Statutory deductions including PF, ESI, TDS, and professional tax shall apply as per Indian labor laws.

4. WORK HOURS AND LOCATION

Work Model: Remote-First (Monthly team meetups)

Standard Working Hours: 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM IST. The Company embraces a remote-first culture with optional co-working space access in Gurgaon. Monthly team collaboration sessions and quarterly offsites are organized for team building and strategic planning. Flexible working hours are encouraged with core collaboration hours from 11:00 AM to 4:00 PM IST.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY & INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

The Employee agrees to maintain strict confidentiality regarding all proprietary information including source code, algorithms, technical architectures, business strategies, client data, financial information, product roadmaps, user analytics, competitive intelligence, internal processes, methodologies, and trade secrets acquired during the course of employment, both during and after termination.

All intellectual property developed during employment shall belong to the Company, including innovations, improvements, and derivative works created using Company resources.

6. NON-COMPETITION & NON-SOLICITATION

For a period of 18 months following the termination of this Agreement, the Employee agrees not to work with or provide services to direct competitors in the AI/tech solutions space, not to solicit Company employees, clients, or partners for competing ventures, and not to use confidential information to develop competing products or services that may harm the interests of the Company.

7. TERMINATION CONDITIONS

Notice Period: Either party may terminate this Agreement with 60 days prior written notice or payment in lieu thereof. The Company reserves the right to terminate employment immediately in cases of gross misconduct, breach of confidentiality, or violation of company policies.

Resignation Policy: If the Employee resigns within the first 12 months of employment, they shall serve the full notice period without the option of payment in lieu. Upon termination, the Employee must return all Company property, complete knowledge transfer activities, and cooperate in the transition process.

8. GOVERNING LAW & DISPUTE RESOLUTION

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the Republic of India. All disputes arising from this Agreement shall be resolved through good faith discussions between parties, binding arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 2015, with Courts in Gurgaon, Haryana having exclusive jurisdiction.
